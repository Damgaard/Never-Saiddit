Take back ownership of your content

If you made it, you should own it. That doesn't just means things in the real world, it means things in the digital world as well. Never Saiddit gives you the ultimate option, to delete your content on Reddit. It's simple and protects your privacy.

Why would you delete your content?

Ultimately, it doesn't matter why you want to remove your content from Reddit. It's your content and you should have the right to choose what happens to it. If you want it to stay, it should stay. If you want it gone, it should be gone. If you're reading this, because you're wondering why people make this choice, then there are a few common reasons

  • You no longer support Reddit as a company, and don't want your content to contribute to their profits
  • The content you made no longer represents who you are as a person.
  • You've shared personal details you no longer feel comfortable being shared.

On Reddit, you can delete your content. But easy, it is not. You have to go to every single piece of content and individually choose to delete it. If you've been on the site for years, this can take hours. Basically it is so inconvinient, that people don't do it. So even though technically, you own your content, in reality you do not.

How does it happen?

How does the deletion happen?

With Reddits API

The process is done via Reddit's own API, so there is no risk of it being stopped in the middle of the process by anti-spam protections.

No passwords shared!

The process is completely done via OAuth, that means your password is never shared with us and can remove access at any time!

No need to be a geek

You just click a few buttons, authorize us and then everything will be handled automatically by us. You don't need to setup a greasemonkey script, run a python script or any other thing that requires technical knowledge.

Complete the process in a single go

Some tools limit you to the last 1000 comments or submissions, requirering the tool to be run over and over again. We do not, run once and the process will be completed in one go.
