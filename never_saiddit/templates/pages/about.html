Reclaim you Content

If you made it, you should own it. That doesn't just means things in the real world, it means things in the digital world as well. Especially now that companies use your content to make money. If you no longer want you content there, you should be able to remove it.

On Reddit, you can delete your content. But easy, it is not. You have to go to every single piece of content and individually choose to delete it. If you've been on the site for years, this can take hours. Basically it is so inconvinient, that people don't do. So even though technically, you own your content, in reality you do not.

Why would you delete your content

Ultimately, it doesn't matter why you want to remove your content from Reddit. It's your content and you should have the right to choose what happens to it. If you're reading this, because you're wondering why people make this choice, then there are a few common reasons

  • You no longer support Reddit as a company, and don't want your content to be part of their profit machine
  • The content you made no longer represents who you are as a person.
  • You've shared personal details you no longer feel comfortable being shared.
